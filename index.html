STEM School Robotics

Welcome to the official website for the STEM School robotics team. Our school's mission is to develop and share a new paradigm for world-class education using technology and as a gateway to cultivate student's inquisitive nature, excerisise innovation, think critically, and collaborate to become leaders who are self sufficent, with the passion of Chattanooga's Renaissance. Through STEM Robotics, students gain a passion to learn, everlasting ability to excersise leadership, and work their minds to come up with innovative and unique ideas.

Teams

Mechanical Team

The mechanical team assembles the body of the robot, on which the other parts are attached. They use our state of the art FABLAB to assemble all the various moving parts that go into making our robots functional, as well as keeping the pneumatics functioning.

Electrical Team

The electrical team works with supplying power to the robots. They plug in and organize all the wires that keep the robots functioning, as well as troubleshooting when things go wrong. Our electrical team members also create and wire the electrical panel, and works with pneumatics.
